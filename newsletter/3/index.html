Newsletter #3 June 2012

 

Well here it is - our new site! It's redesigned, reborn, rejigged, reinvigorated - and most importantly responsive. Responsive? Yes, responsive to the environment you are viewing it in, so for mobile devices it adapts its layout to suit, for tablets it adapts its layout to suit and for your desktop it adapts its layout to suit. And if you're using a desktop browser that isn't IE8 (or less) you can see all the responsive niceness in action, just try resizing your browser and watch the layout adapt. 

Why? Well It means we have one website but multiple different optimised experiences for visitors on different devices. With mobile device usage soon to rival and likely to overtake the desktop as the preferred method of accessing the web - and your website - its important that visitors find the information they need easily and that's where having a responsive / adaptive website bridges the gap. Interesting, yes?

Please get in contact if you're thinking its time to push your site into the mobile world and we can discuss your options.
